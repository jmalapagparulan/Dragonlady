About


Dragonlady Loaning System provides a service of lending money to those who need it, locally.
Formed in the year 2020, Dragonlady has earned a unique reputation for its service.
Serving a large number of borrowers around the area in just a span of months.


Mission

Dragonlady Loaning System is a locally owned, locally loaned community that provides a variety of core financial services to businesses, professionals, and individuals. We promise our customers immediate, direct access to our loaning decision makers and deliver the finest personalized service in the industry. Dragonlady has committed people and resources to enrich the communities where we live and work. Because trust is our most important commodity, we are focused on building and sustaining long-term generational relationships with our customers, our community, our employees, and our shareholders. In every transaction, You Have Us.


Vision

We aspire to become the locally owned, independent community loaning service of choice for small and medium-sized businesses, professionals, and individuals in the Philippines. We will combine steady growth, consistent earnings, and firm control of risk factors to provide safety for our borrowers. Our people will be the difference in establishing consistency in earnings and enhanced shareholder value.

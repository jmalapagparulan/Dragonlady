FAQs

How likely am I to be accepted for a loan?

The only way to find out if you'll be accepted for a loan is to apply. Moreover, the lender will be checking if you have a previous balance, if you don't have, your application for a loan would likely be accepted.


What if my financial circumstances change during a loans term?

Even if your financial circumstances chang during a loans term, you can just approach the lender. The contacts are in the Contact Us section. All information on howw to reach the lender caan be seen under that section.


How often can I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan as long you don't have a current balance that you are not yet paid. It is more likely that your appllication will be disapproved if you have a current unpaid loan.


What is the minimum/maximum loan amount that may be granted to borrowers?

The minimum amount that can be loaned is Php 500, while the maximum amount that can be granted to the borrowers is Php 100,000.


How long do I need to pay the loan (full life of the loan)?

The life of the loan depends on the amount of money you applied for.


For what purposes can the money I borrow be used for?

You can use the money you borrowed for any reasons you deemed necessary to use for. The lender doesn't need to know where you will use the money for.


What are the different loan terms I can apply for?

It varies, but the loan terms can be seen when you try to apply for a loan.


What is the interest rate?

The interest rate is lower compared to most loaning companies. The ratte can also be seen when you ttry to applly for a loan.


What will be my monthly repayment?

The monthly payment will depend on the amount of money you borrowed.


What are my payment options?

Since the payment options improved, we now accept payments via GCash, PayMaya, Paypal, BDO, and of course, the traditional walk-in.


How quickly will I receive the funds if my loan is approved?

Once the loan was approved, you will immediately get a text message that your loan is approved. By that, you will receive the money you need through the mode of loan transfer you inputted when you applied for a loan.
